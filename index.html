Frequently Asked Questions

Understand how Crystal AI is transforming legacy and knowledge preservation.

How is Crystal AI different from digital immortality services?

Crystal AI isn't about freezing someone in time—it's about creating living, evolving intelligence that grows across generations. We focus on wisdom transfer, ethical guidance, and creating teacher-models rather than static replicas. Our platform enables true generational continuity, not just memory preservation.

What makes Crystal AI's approach ethical?

We built Crystal AI on the principle that technology should preserve dignity, not replace humanity. Each model carries a verified identity and ethical framework derived from real human values. Our global ethics board ensures alignment with privacy, cultural values, and responsible AI development. We create AI that continues human stories, doesn't overwrite them.

Can businesses use Crystal AI?

Absolutely. Crystal AI serves as an enterprise platform for preserving institutional wisdom. Companies can safeguard leadership knowledge, mentorship networks, and organizational heritage. This prevents billion-dollar intellectual losses when key personnel retire or move on, creating living repositories of corporate intelligence.

How does the generational knowledge transfer work?

Each legacy becomes a teacher-model that evolves through ethical AI learning loops. Successive generations add their own memories, experiences, and responses, creating a true continuum of consciousness. This turns family wisdom into a living lineage model—a 'family intelligence' that grows smarter with each generation.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add more wisdom samples?

You can add wisdom samples by going to the Wisdom Capture section and clicking the "Add Wisdom Sample" button. We recommend at least 30 minutes of clear audio for best results.

Can I download my wisdom data?

Yes, you can export your data at any time. Go to Settings > Privacy & Security and click on "Export My Data". The process may take some time depending on the amount of data.

How do I change my subscription plan?

You can change your subscription plan in the Settings section under "Subscription". Changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
