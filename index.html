Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Crystal and digital eternity.

How does Crystal differ from other memorial services?

Crystal focuses on celebrating life and preserving memories through interactive technology. We don't approach this as a taboo subject but as a beautiful way to keep the essence of loved ones alive for future generations. Our platform is designed to be uplifting and comforting rather than morbid.

Is the voice cloning process difficult?

Not at all. Our process is designed to be simple and accessible. You just need to provide audio recordings of the person speaking - ideally at least 30 minutes of clear audio. Our AI does the rest, analyzing speech patterns and creating a voice model that can speak any text in their voice.

How private and secure is my data?

Privacy and security are our top priorities. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. You maintain complete control over who can access the digital legacy, and we never share your data with third parties without explicit consent.

Can I update the digital legacy over time?

Absolutely. We encourage families to continue adding memories, stories, and new information over time. This allows the digital legacy to grow and evolve, just as our memories of loved ones do.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add more voice samples?

You can add voice samples by going to the Voice Cloning section and clicking the "Add Voice Sample" button. We recommend at least 30 minutes of clear audio for best results.

Can I download my data?

Yes, you can export your data at any time. Go to Settings > Privacy & Security and click on "Export My Data". The process may take some time depending on the amount of data.

How do I change my subscription plan?

You can change your subscription plan in the Settings section under "Subscription". Changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
